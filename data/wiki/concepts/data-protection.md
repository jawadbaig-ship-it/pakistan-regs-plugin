# Personal Data Protection

*personal data*

## Definition

Statutory and regulatory regime governing processing of personal data — currently anchored by sectoral rules pending the Personal Data Protection Bill 2023.

## Why this matters

Drives consent flows, data-minimisation, breach notification and cross-border-transfer rules.
