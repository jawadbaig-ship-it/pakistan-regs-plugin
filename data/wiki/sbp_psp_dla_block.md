# SBP - Block Digital Payment Services to Unauthorised DLAs (PSD C2 2023)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** PSP&OD Circular No. 02 of 2023 - Digital Payment Services to Unauthorised DLAs  
**Date:** 2023  
**Applicable to:** PSPs, PSOs, banks providing payment-acceptance services  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2023/C2.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2023/C2.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Requires PSPs/PSOs to refuse merchant onboarding and to block payment services to digital lending apps not licensed by SECP/SBP.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Pre-onboarding KYC / licensing check on lending merchants
- Cross-reference against SECP whitelist of permitted DLAs
- Mandatory off-boarding of unlicensed DLAs
- Reporting of unauthorised DLAs to SBP/SECP
- Penalties for facilitating unlicensed digital lending
