# Currency Transaction Report (CTR)

*CTR*

## Definition

Mandatory report to FMU for cash transactions above the prescribed threshold (currently PKR 2 million single / aggregated within a day).

## Why this matters

Threshold-based, not suspicion-based. Automated reporting from core banking + payment systems.
