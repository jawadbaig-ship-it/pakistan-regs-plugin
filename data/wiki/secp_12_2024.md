# SECP Circular 12/2024 - Digital Lending Updates

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Circular 12 of 2024  
**Date:** 2024-05-16  
**Applicable to:** All SECP-licensed Lending NBFCs operating Digital Lending Apps; partners and outsourced service providers  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-no-12-of-2024-requirements-for-nbfcs-engaged-in-digital-lending/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-no-12-of-2024-requirements-for-nbfcs-engaged-in-digital-lending/)

## Purpose & Scope

Updates and consolidates SECP digital-lending requirements (subsumes/refines Circulars 15/2022 and 10/2023). Enables product expansion (e.g., BNPL, secured digital lending, partnership models) within a stricter consumer-protection envelope.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Updated KFS template and disclosure standards
- Expanded list of permissible product variants (BNPL, secured digital loans, partnership lending)
- Self-assessment declaration form for app whitelisting
- Strengthened data-privacy controls (no contact/gallery/SMS scraping)
- Mandatory grievance redressal SLA and SECP reporting
- Outsourcing & partnership rules — NBFC retains principal accountability
- Capital, governance and fit-and-proper expectations for digital-lending NBFCs
