# SBP PSP Customer Notifications for Digital Payments (PSD C1 2024)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** PSP&OD Circular No. 01 of 2024 - Customer Notifications for Digital Payments via Mobile Apps  
**Date:** 2024  
**Applicable to:** PSPs, PSOs, EMIs, Banks, MFBs offering digital payments  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2024/C1.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2024/C1.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Mandates real-time customer notifications for every digital payment - debit and credit, successful and failed - across mobile apps and wallets.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Real-time notification on every debit and credit
- Notification fields - amount, beneficiary, channel, balance, reference
- Multi-channel delivery (push + SMS fallback)
- No opt-out for transaction alerts
- Failure-handling and reversal notifications
- Audit log retention and SBP inspection
