# SBP Licensing & Regulatory Framework for Digital Banks

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular No. 01 of 2022 — Licensing & Regulatory Framework for Digital Banks  
**Date:** 2022-01-03  
**Applicable to:** Sponsors applying for a Digital Bank licence, and licensed Digital Banks  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/C1-Annex.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/C1-Annex.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Creates a dedicated licensing regime for fully-digital banks (no/limited branches). Two categories: Digital Retail Bank (DRB) and Digital Full Bank (DFB). Phased rollout via Pilot then Commercial stages.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Section I — Interpretations & definitions
- Section II — General provisions (licence types, sponsors, suspension/revocation)
- Section III — Application process & licensing stages for DRB
- Pre-Application, NOC, In-Principle Approval, Pilot, Commercial
- Minimum Paid-Up Capital — DRB PKR 1.5bn (raised to PKR 4bn in commercial stage); DFB higher
- Fit & Proper Criteria for sponsors, BoD, and key executives
- Governance — Board composition, independent directors, committees
- Risk management, cybersecurity, technology architecture, BCP
- Customer protection, AML/CFT, complaint handling
- Reporting, supervision and on-site inspection
