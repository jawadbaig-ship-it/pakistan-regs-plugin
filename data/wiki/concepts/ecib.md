# Electronic Credit Information Bureau (eCIB)

*eCIB*

## Definition

SBP-operated credit information bureau that aggregates borrower data from all member FIs. Pre-disbursement check is mandatory; monthly reporting is mandatory; data is confidential under Section 25-A BCO 1962.

## Why this matters

Cannot lend in Pakistan without an eCIB membership and check. Underwriting must consume eCIB data; collections must report DPD back monthly.
