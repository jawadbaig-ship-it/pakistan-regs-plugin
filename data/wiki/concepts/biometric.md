# Biometric Verification

*biometric*

## Definition

Fingerprint-based or liveness-based verification of natural-person identity, typically via NADRA BVS or in-app liveness check (selfie + motion).

## Why this matters

Mandatory step under Consolidated Onboarding Framework for opening accounts, issuing wallets, and elevating account tiers.
