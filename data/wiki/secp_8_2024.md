# SECP Circular 8/2024 - Digital Lending Guidelines

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Circular 8 of 2024  
**Date:** 2024-03-18  
**Applicable to:** All SECP-licensed Lending NBFCs running Digital Lending Apps (DLAs); third-party service providers acting on their behalf  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-8-of-2024-guidelines-for-nbfcs-engaged-in-digital-lending-2/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-8-of-2024-guidelines-for-nbfcs-engaged-in-digital-lending-2/)

## Purpose & Scope

Consolidates earlier SECP digital-lending guidance and codifies the operating standard for licensed lending-NBFCs that originate, service or recover loans through digital channels (apps, web). Issued under Section 282B of the Companies Ordinance / NBFC Rules.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Eligibility — only licensed lending NBFCs may operate DLAs (no unlicensed apps)
- App-store whitelisting (Google/Apple) — DLA must be on SECP whitelist
- Mandatory Key Fact Statement (KFS) before loan disbursal — APR, tenor, EMI schedule, all fees, total cost
- Prohibition on harvesting phone contacts, photo gallery, media (irrespective of consent)
- Required disclosures of partners, lenders, helpline, complaint email
- Recovery/collection conduct rules — no harassment, no third-party contact without consent
- Data privacy: explicit, granular consent; data minimisation; retention limits
- Cybersecurity & app integrity controls; periodic VAPT
- Complaint handling TAT and SECP/SBP escalation linkage
