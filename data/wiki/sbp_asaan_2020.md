# SBP Asaan Digital / Asaan Mobile Account Guidelines

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular Letter 16 of 2020 - Asaan Digital Account / Asaan Mobile Account  
**Date:** 2020  
**Applicable to:** Banks, MFBs (including Digital Banks via separate framework)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2020/CL16.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2020/CL16.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Low-friction account product to drive financial inclusion - simplified CDD, smaller transaction limits, fully digital opening using NADRA Verisys + biometric/OTP.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Eligibility - Pakistani CNIC holder, individual, low-income segment
- Simplified KYC requirements; NADRA/biometric verification
- Transaction and balance limits (Asaan / Asaan Digital / Asaan Mobile)
- Permissible features - inward/outward transfers, bill payments, salary credits
- Upgrade path to full account upon completion of full CDD
- Reporting to SBP
