# SECP strengthens measures to prevent predatory lending in Digital Nano-Lending (Press Release)

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Press Release Aug 2023  
**Date:** 2023-08-07  
**Applicable to:** Industry stakeholders  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/Press-Release-Aug-7-SECP-strengthens-measures-to-prevent-predatory-lending-in-Digital-Nano-Lending.pdf](https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/Press-Release-Aug-7-SECP-strengthens-measures-to-prevent-predatory-lending-in-Digital-Nano-Lending.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Press release accompanying Circular 10/2023 - imposes mathematical caps on nano-lending exposure, tenor and APR.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Caps on exposure, tenor, APR
- Cooling-off and rollover restrictions
- Enforcement and complaint mechanism
