# Risk Management Guidelines for Commercial Banks & DFIs

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Risk Management Guidelines  
**Date:** 2003 (foundational)  
**Applicable to:** Banks and DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/riskmgm.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/riskmgm.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Foundational SBP guidance on enterprise risk management - credit, market, liquidity, operational, legal/compliance and strategic risks. Pre-cursor to current TGRMF.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Credit Risk Management - origination, structuring, monitoring
- Market Risk - interest rate, FX, equity
- Liquidity Risk - LCR/NSFR precursor
- Operational Risk - controls, KRIs, BCM
- Legal & Compliance risk
- Risk governance - Board, RMC, CRO
