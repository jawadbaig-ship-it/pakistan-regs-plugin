# Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA), 2016

**Regulator:** GoP / MoITT  
**Reference:** PECA 2016  
**Date:** 2016 (amended)  
**Applicable to:** All persons; relevant for FI cyber-incident reporting and customer fraud  
**Source:** [https://na.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1470910659_707.pdf](https://na.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1470910659_707.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Cybercrime statute - criminalises unauthorised access, electronic fraud, identity theft, hate speech online and lays groundwork for digital evidence.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Offences against confidentiality, integrity and availability of data/systems
- Electronic fraud and forgery
- Identity theft and unauthorised use of identity information
- Cyber-stalking and harassment
- Powers of investigation - FIA Cyber Wing
- International cooperation, MLAT
- Penalties and procedure
