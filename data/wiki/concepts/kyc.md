# Know Your Customer (KYC)

*Know Your Customer*

## Definition

Operational layer of CDD — identity verification (CNIC + biometric/NADRA Verisys), address proof, beneficial-owner identification, screening against sanctions and PEP lists.

## Why this matters

Must occur before any financial relationship; tiered (Asaan / standard / EDD) by product risk.
