# Basel Capital Adequacy Framework Instructions for Credit Risk

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD C3/2025 Annex A  
**Date:** 2025  
**Applicable to:** Banks and DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2025/C3-AnnexA.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2025/C3-AnnexA.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Latest SBP instructions on the credit-risk component of Basel capital - standardised approach, IRB approach, securitisation, credit risk mitigation and disclosures.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Standardised Approach (SA) for Credit Risk
- External credit assessments and ECAIs
- Internal Ratings-Based (IRB) Approach
- Credit Risk Mitigation (CRM) techniques
- Securitisation framework
- Counterparty Credit Risk
- Pillar 3 disclosures specific to credit risk
