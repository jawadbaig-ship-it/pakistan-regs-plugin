# SBP Basel III Implementation

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Basel Capital Standards - Basel III implementation  
**Date:** 2013 (BPRD C8/2013)  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2013/C8.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2013/C8.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Implements Basel III capital and liquidity standards in Pakistan - CET1, Tier-1, Total Capital ratios, capital conservation and counter-cyclical buffers, leverage ratio, LCR/NSFR.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- CET1, Additional Tier-1, Tier-2 instruments and eligibility
- Minimum capital ratios (CET1 6%, T1 7.5%, Total 11.5% incl. CCB)
- Capital Conservation Buffer (2.5%)
- Counter-cyclical Capital Buffer
- D-SIB framework (additional capital surcharge)
- Leverage Ratio
- Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) and Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR)
- Disclosures (Pillar 3)
- Transitional arrangements and phase-in
