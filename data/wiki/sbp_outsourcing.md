# SBP Outsourcing Risk Management Framework

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular No. 06 of 2019 — Risk Management in Outsourcing Arrangements  
**Date:** 2019  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2019/C6-Annex-II.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2019/C6-Annex-II.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Requires SBP REs to identify, manage and supervise risks arising from outsourcing of business or technology functions. Material outsourcing requires prior SBP no-objection.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of material outsourcing
- Board and senior management responsibilities — outsourcing policy, register, approval matrix
- Risk assessment and due diligence on service provider (financial, operational, reputational)
- Mandatory contract clauses — audit rights, SBP access, confidentiality, exit
- Sub-contracting controls
- Cross-border outsourcing requirements
- Ongoing monitoring, KPIs, periodic review
- Business continuity & exit strategy
- Prior SBP approval threshold for material outsourcing
- Customer data protection and information security
