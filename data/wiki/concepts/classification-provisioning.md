# Loan Classification & Provisioning

*classification*

## Definition

DPD-bucket classification (OAEM, Substandard, Doubtful, Loss) with associated minimum provisioning percentages.

## Why this matters

Drives both regulatory and IFRS-9 provisioning; cross-checked with eCIB reporting.
