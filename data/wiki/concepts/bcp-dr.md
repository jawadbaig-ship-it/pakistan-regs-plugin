# Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery

*BCP*

## Definition

Plans, sites and runbooks ensuring continuity of critical services through outages, cyber incidents and disasters.

## Why this matters

Tested annually; RTO/RPO documented; coordinated with cloud/outsourcing arrangements.
