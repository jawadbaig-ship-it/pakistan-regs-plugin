# SECP NBFC & Notified Entities Regulations 2008

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** NBFC & Notified Entities Regulations, 2008  
**Date:** 2008 (with amendments)  
**Applicable to:** All NBFCs and notified entities  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/regulations/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/regulations/)

## Purpose & Scope

Detailed operating regulations supplementing the 2003 Rules - covers risk management, exposure norms, classification, capital adequacy and product-level rules for NBFCs.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Capital adequacy and minimum equity by category
- Exposure limits per borrower / per group / sector
- Connected-party and related-party transactions
- Classification & provisioning of advances
- Investment limits and restrictions
- Borrowing limits (debt-to-equity)
- Reporting, audit, fit & proper
- Specific regimes - leasing, IF, AM, REIT, NBMFC, VCC
