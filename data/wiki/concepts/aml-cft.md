# AML/CFT/CPF Framework

*AML*

## Definition

Pakistan's anti-money-laundering, counter-financing-of-terrorism, and counter-proliferation-financing regime — anchored in AML Act 2010, FMU oversight, and sectoral regs (SBP for banks/MFBs/Digital Banks/EMIs; SECP for NBFCs).

## Why this matters

Cross-cutting compliance area touching onboarding, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, STR/CTR reporting, governance and training.
