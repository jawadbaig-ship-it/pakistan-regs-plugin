# Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

*Customer Due Diligence*

## Definition

Risk-based identification and verification of customers, purpose of relationship and source of funds. Includes simplified CDD (low-risk), standard CDD, and enhanced due diligence (EDD) for higher-risk customers and PEPs.

## Why this matters

Foundation of AML/CFT compliance. Failure to perform CDD is a primary AML offence under AML Act 2010.
