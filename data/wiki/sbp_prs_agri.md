# Prudential Regulations for Agriculture Financing

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Publications Portal  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** Banks and DFIs offering agriculture finance  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/index.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/index.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Prudential framework for lending to agricultural entities - production, development, agribusiness, and indicative per-acre credit limits.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of agriculture entity
- Indicative per-acre credit limits by crop
- Eligible securities (incl. crop hypothecation)
- Classification, provisioning, rescheduling
- Interaction with State Crop Insurance / Risk-sharing schemes
- SBP refinance schemes for agri
