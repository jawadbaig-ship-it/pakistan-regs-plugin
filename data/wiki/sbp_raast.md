# SBP Raast - Pakistan Instant Payment System

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** Raast - Pakistans Instant Payment System  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** All Banks, MFBs, EMIs as Raast participants; merchants and consumers as users  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/PS/Raast.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/PS/Raast.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

SBP-operated real-time, 24x7, low-cost retail payment rail - person-to-person, person-to-merchant and bulk/government payments using Raast IDs (alias-based) and ISO 20022 messaging.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Raast scheme rulebook - participant onboarding
- Raast ID (alias) registration via mobile/CNIC
- P2P, P2M, P2G, G2P payment flows
- Pricing - free for retail P2P; merchant pricing tiered
- Settlement - real-time gross settlement on SBP books
- Dispute handling and chargeback rules
- Technical specs - ISO 20022 messages, APIs
- Fraud and risk monitoring
