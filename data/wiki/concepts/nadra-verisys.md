# NADRA Verisys / BVS

*Verisys*

## Definition

NADRA-operated identity-verification services — Verisys (CNIC verification API) and BVS (Biometric Verification System using fingerprint). Backbone of digital onboarding in Pakistan.

## Why this matters

Practically every digital-onboarding flow integrates Verisys + BVS. Service-level and audit-trail obligations apply.
