# SBP Shariah Governance Framework for Islamic Banking

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Shariah Governance Framework for Islamic Banking Institutions  
**Date:** Active  
**Applicable to:** Full-fledged Islamic Banks, IBIs (Islamic windows of conventional banks)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/ibd/index.asp](https://www.sbp.org.pk/ibd/index.asp)

## Purpose & Scope

Governance framework for Islamic Banking Institutions - structure, roles and responsibilities of Shariah Board, Resident Shariah Board Member, Shariah Compliance Department and Shariah Audit, plus product approval process.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Composition and qualifications of the Shariah Board (SB)
- Role of Resident Shariah Board Member (RSBM)
- Shariah Compliance Department - independence and reporting
- Internal Shariah Audit
- External Shariah Audit
- Product development and Shariah approval process
- Profit & loss distribution policies (Mudaraba pools)
- Customer disclosures and complaint handling
- Reporting to SBP
