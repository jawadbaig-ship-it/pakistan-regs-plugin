# SECP AML/CFT Regulations for NBFCs

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP AML/CFT Regulations for Regulated Persons  
**Date:** AML/CFT Regs (current)  
**Applicable to:** All SECP-regulated persons including NBFCs (lending NBFCs, NBMFCs, AMCs, IFCs)  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/regulations/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/regulations/)

## Purpose & Scope

AML/CFT regime for SECP-regulated entities including NBFCs - aligned with Pakistan AML Act 2010 and FATF Recommendations.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Risk-based approach and enterprise risk assessment
- CDD / EDD / SDD
- Beneficial ownership identification
- Sanctions and UNSC screening
- PEPs and high-risk jurisdictions
- STR / CTR reporting to FMU
- Record keeping (5+ years)
- Internal controls, training, audit, designated AML officer
- Penalties for non-compliance
