# SECP Circular 10/2023 - Digital Nano-Lending

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Circular 10 of 2023  
**Date:** 2023-08-07  
**Applicable to:** Lending NBFCs providing digital nano-lending (small-ticket, short-tenor consumer loans through DLAs)  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-no-10-of-2023-requirements-for-lending-nbfcs-providing-digital-nano-lending/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/circular-no-10-of-2023-requirements-for-lending-nbfcs-providing-digital-nano-lending/)

## Purpose & Scope

Imposes mathematical constraints on the digital nano-lending product to prevent debt traps and predatory practices. Establishes hard limits that must be encoded into the underwriting engine.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of nano-loan: small-ticket short-tenor unsecured consumer loan
- Maximum aggregate exposure caps per borrower (across all NBFC nano-loans)
- Tenor cap (typically up to ~90 days) and rollover restrictions
- Cap on total fees + markup (APR ceiling) and prohibition on hidden charges
- Cooling-off period before re-lending to the same borrower
- Mandatory Key Fact Statement (KFS) prior to disbursement
- eCIB / private credit bureau check before approval
- Restrictions on collection practices and contact frequency
