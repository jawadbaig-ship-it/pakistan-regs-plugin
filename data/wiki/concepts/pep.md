# Politically Exposed Person (PEP)

*PEP*

## Definition

Individual entrusted with prominent public functions (foreign or domestic) and their family/close associates. Triggers EDD, senior-management approval and source-of-funds scrutiny.

## Why this matters

PEP screening + EDD must be integrated into onboarding and monitored across the relationship lifecycle.
