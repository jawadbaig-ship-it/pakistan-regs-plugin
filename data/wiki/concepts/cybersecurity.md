# Cybersecurity

*cyber*

## Definition

Defensive controls against cyber threats — VAPT, threat intelligence, incident response, CISO function, encryption, access control, secure SDLC.

## Why this matters

Sectoral CII status under National Cyber Security Policy; SBP and SECP both impose mandatory controls.
