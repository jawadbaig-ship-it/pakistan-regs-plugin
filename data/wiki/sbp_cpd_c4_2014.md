# CPD Circular No. 04/2014

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** CPD Circular 04 of 2014  
**Date:** 2014-08-29  
**Applicable to:** Banks, MFBs, DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2014/C4.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2014/C4.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Foundational consumer-protection circular - precursor to the Business Conduct & Fair Treatment Regulatory Framework (BC&FRF).

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Fair conduct expectations
- Disclosure standards (KFS pre-cursor)
- Complaint handling commitments
- Linkage with Banking Mohtasib
