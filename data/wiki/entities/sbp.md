# State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)

*State Bank of Pakistan*

## Definition

Central bank of Pakistan and primary regulator for banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs, PSOs and PSPs. Issues prudential, conduct, AML/CFT, technology and consumer-protection regulation.
