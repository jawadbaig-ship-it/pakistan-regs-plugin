# Payment Systems & Electronic Fund Transfers (PS&EFT) Act, 2007

**Regulator:** GoP  
**Reference:** PS&EFT Act 2007  
**Date:** 2007  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, PSOs/PSPs, EMIs, customers  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2007/EFT_Act_2007.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2007/EFT_Act_2007.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Statutory basis for SBPs oversight of payment systems and PSO/PSP/EMI licensing; legal recognition of EFTs, finality of settlement, customer rights and obligations.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Designation and oversight of payment systems by SBP
- PSO/PSP licensing and supervision
- Recognition of electronic funds transfers and settlement finality
- Rights and liabilities of payer, payee and PSP
- Authentication and unauthorised transactions
- Dispute resolution and complaint mechanism
- Offences and penalties
