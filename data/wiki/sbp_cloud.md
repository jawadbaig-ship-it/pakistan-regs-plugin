# SBP Framework on Outsourcing to CSPs

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular 01 of 2023 — Framework on Outsourcing to Cloud Service Providers  
**Date:** 2023  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs (REs)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2023/C1-Annix-A.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2023/C1-Annix-A.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Specialised overlay on the outsourcing framework permitting REs to use public/private/hybrid cloud, subject to risk categorisation, due diligence, data localisation and SBP approval.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Scope — material vs non-material workloads on cloud
- Data classification and localisation requirements (sensitive data must remain onshore)
- CSP due diligence (certifications: ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI-DSS where relevant)
- Mandatory contract clauses — SBP audit rights, data residency, breach notification
- Encryption (in-transit and at-rest), key management
- Concentration risk — limits on dependence on single CSP/region
- Exit strategy and reversibility plan
- Incident reporting and BCP/DR
- SBP may instruct shifting workloads to onshore CSPs
- Continuous monitoring and periodic risk reassessment
