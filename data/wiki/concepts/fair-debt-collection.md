# Fair Debt Collection

*fair debt*

## Definition

Conduct rules for collection and recovery against borrowers — minimum 14-day notice, no harassment, allowed contact hours, identity disclosure, recorded calls, agency enrolment with PBA.

## Why this matters

Operationalised by BPRD C13/2008 and PBA 2021 guidelines; SECP imposes parallel obligations on NBFCs.
