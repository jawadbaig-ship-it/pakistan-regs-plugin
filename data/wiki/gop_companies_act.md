# Companies Act, 2017

**Regulator:** GoP  
**Reference:** Companies Act 2017  
**Date:** 2017  
**Applicable to:** All companies including Banks, DFIs, MFBs, NBFCs, EMIs  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/acts/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/acts/)

## Purpose & Scope

Primary corporate-law statute - incorporation, governance, disclosures, mergers and winding up. NBFCs and Banks (as companies) must comply with Companies Act in addition to sectoral law.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Incorporation and types of companies
- Share capital, debentures, shareholders rights
- Directors duties, board governance, related-party transactions
- Financial reporting and audit (incl. IFRS)
- Mergers, amalgamations, takeovers (Section 282-series for NBFCs)
- Winding up and corporate insolvency
- SECP enforcement powers
