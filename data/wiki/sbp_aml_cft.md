# SBP AML/CFT/CPF Regulations

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular Letter 33/2022 — AML/CFT/CPF Regulations for SBP REs  
**Date:** 2022 (Consolidated)  
**Applicable to:** All SBP-regulated entities — Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs, exchange companies  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/CL33-Annex-B.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/CL33-Annex-B.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Single AML/CFT/CPF regulatory framework binding all SBP regulated entities, aligned with FATF recommendations and the AML Act 2010.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part A — Definitions; Part B — Applicability; Part C — Regulations
- Enterprise-wide ML/TF/PF risk assessment
- CDD: identification, verification (NADRA), purpose & nature of relationship
- EDD for higher-risk customers, PEPs, correspondent banking
- Beneficial ownership identification (≥25% threshold)
- Sanctions & UNSC screening (real-time, name-by-name, transaction-level)
- STR / CTR / FT-STR reporting to FMU within prescribed timelines
- Record keeping (5+ years)
- Reliance on third parties / group reliance
- Independent audit function and AML training
- Governance — designated Compliance Officer, board oversight
