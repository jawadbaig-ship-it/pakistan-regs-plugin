# UN / Domestic Sanctions

*sanctions*

## Definition

Targeted financial sanctions implementing UN Security Council Resolutions (1267, 1373, 1718, 2231) and domestic Schedules under the AML Act — REs must screen names and freeze assets.

## Why this matters

Real-time screening at onboarding and per-transaction; lists update frequently and have no grace period.
