# IFRS-9 / Expected Credit Loss

*IFRS*

## Definition

Forward-looking expected-credit-loss (ECL) provisioning model in three stages (Stage 1 / 2 / 3) with SICR triggers — replaces incurred-loss model.

## Why this matters

Provisioning is the higher of IFRS-9 ECL and SBP PR-classification provisioning.
