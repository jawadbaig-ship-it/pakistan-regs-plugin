# SBP IFRS-9 Implementation for Banks

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP IFRS-9 Implementation Instructions  
**Date:** 2022 (Implementation)  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs (later phased to MFBs)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/IFRS9.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/IFRS9.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Mandates IFRS-9 expected-credit-loss (ECL) provisioning for banks - replacing incurred-loss model. Three-stage classification with 12-month or lifetime ECL based on significant increase in credit risk (SICR).

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Scope and effective date
- Three-stage ECL model (Stage 1 / Stage 2 / Stage 3)
- SICR criteria and triggers
- PD / LGD / EAD modelling guidance
- Forward-looking macroeconomic overlays
- Interaction with PRs classification (the higher provisioning applies)
- Disclosure requirements
- Governance - ECL committee, independent validation
- Transition adjustments
