# Electronic Transactions Ordinance (ETO), 2002

**Regulator:** GoP  
**Reference:** ETO XLI of 2002  
**Date:** 2002  
**Applicable to:** All persons transacting electronically (incl. FIs, fintechs, NADRA)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/l_frame/ETO_2002.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/l_frame/ETO_2002.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Foundational statute giving legal validity to electronic documents, signatures and contracts in Pakistan; basis for digital onboarding, e-signature and digital lending agreements.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Legal recognition of electronic documents and signatures
- Attribution of electronic communications
- Time and place of dispatch and receipt
- Certification Service Providers (CSPs) and accreditation
- Admissibility of electronic evidence
- Offences relating to electronic forgery
