# Microfinance Institutions Ordinance, 2001

**Regulator:** GoP  
**Reference:** MFI Ordinance LV of 2001  
**Date:** 2001  
**Applicable to:** Microfinance Banks (MFBs) licensed by SBP  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/acd/MFI-Ordinance-2001.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/acd/MFI-Ordinance-2001.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Establishes the legal framework for Microfinance Banks in Pakistan - separate from BCO regime to enable focused supply of financial services to the poor.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of poor person and microfinance services
- Licensing, capital and area of operations (Provincial / National)
- Permissible business and product set
- Deposit-taking authority and depositor protection
- Governance - Board, management, fit & proper
- Reporting and supervision
- Penalties and dissolution
