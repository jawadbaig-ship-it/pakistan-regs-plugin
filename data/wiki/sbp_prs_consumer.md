# SBP Prudential Regulations - Consumer Financing

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Prudential Regulations for Consumer Financing  
**Date:** Updated periodically (current consolidated)  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs offering consumer finance to individuals  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-Consumer.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-Consumer.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Sets prudential limits for consumer lending — personal loans, credit cards, auto loans, housing finance — including affordability, exposure, classification, provisioning and disclosure rules.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part A — Definitions (Bank, Borrower, Consumer Financing, DPD, Equity, FI)
- Part B — Prudential limits: Debt Burden Ratio (DBR) cap, exposure limits per borrower / per product
- Part C — Personal loans: max amount, tenor, clean vs secured
- Credit cards: limits, billing disclosure, finance charges, KFS
- Auto loans: down-payment, IDC (insurance), tenor caps
- Housing finance: LTV, DSCR, refinance markup
- Mandatory eCIB check pre-approval
- Classification & provisioning of consumer NPLs (DPD buckets)
- Disclosure: Schedule of Charges (SOC), Key Fact Statement (KFS), markup recalculation rules
