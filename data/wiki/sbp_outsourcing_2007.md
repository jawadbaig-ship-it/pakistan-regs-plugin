# SBP Guidelines on Outsourcing Arrangements (2007)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Annex C9 of 2007  
**Date:** 2007  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2007/annex_c9.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2007/annex_c9.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Original SBP outsourcing guidance - superseded by BPRD C6/2019 (Tier 2) but retained for historical reference and continuing principles.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of outsourcing
- Pre-outsourcing assessment and Board approval
- Service-provider due diligence
- Mandatory contract terms and audit rights
- Cross-border outsourcing
- Confidentiality and data protection
