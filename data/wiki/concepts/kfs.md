# Key Fact Statement (KFS)

*Key Fact Statement*

## Definition

Standardised pre-disbursement disclosure showing approved amount, APR, tenor, EMI schedule with dates, all fees, total cost of credit, and key terms — must be acknowledged by the borrower before funds are released.

## Why this matters

Hardcoded into every loan-origination flow that lends in Pakistan. Required by both SECP (digital lending) and SBP (consumer financing).
