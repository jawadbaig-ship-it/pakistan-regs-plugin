# SBP Regulatory Sandbox Framework

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Regulatory Sandbox Framework  
**Date:** 2025 (Refreshed)  
**Applicable to:** Licensed REs and unlicensed innovators (subject to entry criteria)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2025/Regulatory-Sandbox.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2025/Regulatory-Sandbox.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Time-bound, controlled environment for testing innovative financial products, services or business models with relaxed/customised regulatory expectations and exit criteria.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Eligibility and entry criteria (genuine innovation, consumer benefit, risk controls)
- Cohort-based application windows
- Testing parameters - customer cap, transaction cap, duration
- Customer protections during testing (consent, disclosures, limits)
- Reporting cadence to SBP during the sandbox period
- Exit options - full licensing, modified licensing, exit
- Public list of sandbox participants
