# Prevention Against Cyber Attacks - SBP Instructions

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD C7 of 2016  
**Date:** 2016  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2016/C7.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2016/C7.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Sectoral cyber-defence directives - mandates threat-intel sharing, incident response, periodic VAPT and Board-level cyber oversight.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Threat intelligence and information sharing
- Mandatory incident response plan
- Periodic VAPT and red-teaming
- Patch management discipline
- CISO appointment and Board reporting
- Reporting cyber incidents to SBP within timelines
