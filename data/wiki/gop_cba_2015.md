# Credit Bureaus Act, 2015

**Regulator:** GoP  
**Reference:** Credit Bureaus Act 2015  
**Date:** 2015  
**Applicable to:** Private Credit Bureaus (e.g., Tasdeeq, DataCheck), member FIs, consumers  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/CBA-2015.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/CBA-2015.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Establishes the licensing, supervision and operating regime for private credit bureaus in Pakistan; complements SBPs eCIB and codifies borrower data rights.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Licensing and capital requirements for private bureaus
- Permissible business and data sources
- Mandatory data sharing by member FIs
- Borrower consent, dispute resolution and right to free annual report
- Confidentiality, data security and retention
- Penalties and SBP supervisory powers
