# SECP NBFC (Establishment & Regulation) Rules 2003

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** NBFC (Establishment & Regulation) Rules, 2003  
**Date:** 2003 (with amendments)  
**Applicable to:** All NBFCs (lending, leasing, investment finance, asset management, micro-finance NBFCs etc.)  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/rules/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/laws/rules/)

## Purpose & Scope

Primary delegated legislation under the Companies Ordinance / Act and the SBP Act governing NBFC formation, licensing, business scope and basic prudential requirements.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definition of NBFC and types - Lending, Leasing, Investment Finance, Discount House, Housing Finance, Venture Capital, Investment Advisor, Asset Management, REIT Mgmt, NBMFC
- Licensing - eligibility, fit & proper, capital, NOC
- Permissible activities by NBFC type
- Minimum equity / paid-up capital by category
- Restrictions on dealings (related parties, exposures)
- Reporting and disclosure
- Suspension, cancellation, liquidation
