# National Cyber Security Policy, 2021

**Regulator:** GoP / MoITT  
**Reference:** National Cyber Security Policy 2021  
**Date:** 2021  
**Applicable to:** All entities - especially FIs as Critical Information Infrastructure (CII)  
**Source:** [https://moitt.gov.pk/](https://moitt.gov.pk/)

## Purpose & Scope

National-level cyber-security strategy - critical infrastructure protection, sectoral CERTs, capacity building, public-private cooperation. FIs are designated CII.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Strategic objectives and pillars
- Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) protection
- Sectoral CERTs (incl. financial sector)
- Capacity building and skills
- International cooperation
