# SBP Branchless Banking Regulations (Updated 2019)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular No. 10 of 2019 - Branchless Banking Regulations  
**Date:** 2019 (Updated)  
**Applicable to:** Banks and MFBs operating Branchless Banking and Mobile-Wallet products  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2019/C10-Branchless-Banking-Regulations.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2019/C10-Branchless-Banking-Regulations.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Regulatory framework for delivery of financial services through agents and electronic channels (Level 0/1/2 mobile-wallet accounts), enabling financial inclusion under risk-based KYC tiers.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- 1. Background & Objectives
- 2. Permissible models - bank-led / agent-led
- 3. Permissible activities - cash-in/out, P2P, P2M, bill payment, government payments, IBFT
- 4. Risk assessment and mitigation
- Account categories - Level 0 (low-KYC), Level 1, Level 2
- Transaction limits per account level (daily/monthly/yearly)
- KYC/CDD - NADRA verification, biometric, OTP
- Agent due diligence and oversight responsibilities
- AML/CFT - STR triggers, monitoring
- Consumer protection, complaint handling, transaction notifications
