# SBP Prudential Regulations for MFBs (2025)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Prudential Regulations for Microfinance Banks (2025)  
**Date:** 2025  
**Applicable to:** All Microfinance Banks licensed under MFI Ordinance 2001  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-Microfinance-Banks-2025.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-Microfinance-Banks-2025.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Prudential framework for MFBs — combining microfinance social objectives (poor-person lending, financial inclusion) with prudential safety (capital, liquidity, classification).

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definitions — poor person, microenterprise, small loan thresholds
- CRR / SLR — rates, eligible assets/liabilities, penalties
- Capital adequacy and minimum paid-up capital by category (Provincial / National)
- Maximum loan size per borrower by product (general, housing, micro-enterprise)
- Aggregate exposure limits (per borrower, per group, related parties)
- Deposit mobilisation rules and depositor protection
- Classification & provisioning (DPD buckets specific to MFB book)
- Governance — Board, Risk Management, Audit, Compliance
- Consumer protection — KFS, Schedule of Charges, complaint handling
- Branchless banking and digital channels
