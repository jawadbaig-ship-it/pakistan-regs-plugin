# State Bank of Pakistan Act, 1956

**Regulator:** GoP / SBP  
**Reference:** Act XXXIII of 1956  
**Date:** 1956 (amended 2022)  
**Applicable to:** Government, SBP, all SBP-regulated entities  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/l_frame/SBP_Act.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/l_frame/SBP_Act.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Establishes SBP as Pakistans central bank with statutory authority over monetary policy, payment systems, currency issuance and prudential regulation of banks/DFIs/MFBs/Digital Banks/EMIs.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Establishment, capital and constitution of SBP
- Functions and powers of SBP (note issuance, banker to government and banks)
- Monetary Policy Committee
- Foreign exchange reserves management
- Supervision of banking system
- Payment systems oversight
- Penalties and offences
- Autonomy and accountability provisions (post-2022 amendment)
