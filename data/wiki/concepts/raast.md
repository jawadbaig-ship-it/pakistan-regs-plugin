# Raast Instant Payments

*Raast*

## Definition

SBP-operated 24×7 retail payment rail using ISO 20022 messaging and alias-based Raast IDs for P2P, P2M, P2G and G2P transactions.

## Why this matters

Integration is mandatory for banks/MFBs offering retail accounts; pricing rules cap merchant MDR.
