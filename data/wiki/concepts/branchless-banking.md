# Branchless Banking & Mobile Wallets

*branchless banking*

## Definition

Delivery of financial services through agents and electronic channels with risk-tiered KYC (Level 0/1/2 mobile-wallet accounts).

## Why this matters

The original digital-inclusion rail in Pakistan; today coexists with EMIs and Digital Banks.
