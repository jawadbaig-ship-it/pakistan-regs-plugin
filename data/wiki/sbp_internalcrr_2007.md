# Guidelines on Internal Credit Risk Rating Systems

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BSRVD Annex C8 of 2007  
**Date:** 2007  
**Applicable to:** Banks and DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bsrvd/2007/Annex-C8.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bsrvd/2007/Annex-C8.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Sets minimum standards for FIs internal credit-risk rating frameworks - rating philosophy, scale calibration, validation and use in pricing/provisioning.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Purpose and scope of internal rating systems
- Rating philosophy (PIT vs TTC)
- Scale design and calibration
- Rating process governance and independence
- Validation - quantitative and qualitative
- Use in pricing, limits and provisioning
- IT infrastructure and audit
