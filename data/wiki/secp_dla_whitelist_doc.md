# Digital Lending Apps run/administered by Licensed NBFCs (Whitelist)

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Document - Digital Lending Apps  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** Licensed lending NBFCs, Google Play, PSPs  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/digital-lending-apps-being-run-and-administered-by-duly-licensed-lending-nbfcs/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/document/digital-lending-apps-being-run-and-administered-by-duly-licensed-lending-nbfcs/)

## Purpose & Scope

Authoritative whitelist of permitted Digital Lending Apps run by SECP-licensed NBFCs - basis for Google Play Store enforcement and PSP merchant onboarding.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Whitelisted DLA names and parent NBFC
- Self-assessment declaration linkage (per Circular 12/2024)
- Periodic refresh and removal
