# FMU - Financial Monitoring Unit Portal

**Regulator:** FMU  
**Reference:** FMU Portal  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** All Reporting Entities under AML Act 2010  
**Source:** [https://www.fmu.gov.pk/](https://www.fmu.gov.pk/)

## Purpose & Scope

Pakistans FIU portal - goAML access, STR/CTR guidance, sanctions notifications, legal framework, NRA findings.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- goAML registration and reporting
- Guidance on STR/CTR
- Sanctions notifications and screening lists
- National Risk Assessment publications
- Outreach and training
