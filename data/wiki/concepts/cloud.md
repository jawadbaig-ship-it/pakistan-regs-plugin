# Cloud / CSP Outsourcing

*cloud*

## Definition

Use of public, private or hybrid cloud infrastructure (CSPs) for material workloads — overlaid on the general outsourcing framework with specific data-residency, encryption and exit requirements.

## Why this matters

Sensitive customer data must remain onshore; SBP can instruct repatriation. Concentration risk on a single CSP is supervised.
