# SBP Complaint Resolution Mechanism

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP CPD Portal  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** Consumers and FI consumer-protection units  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/cpd-mech.asp](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/cpd-mech.asp)

## Purpose & Scope

Consumer-facing portal explaining how to escalate a complaint - first to FI, then to SBP CPD, and finally to Banking Mohtasib.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Step 1 - FI complaint cell
- Step 2 - SBP CPD escalation
- Step 3 - Banking Mohtasib
- Web complaint form and contact channels
- TAT expectations at each step
