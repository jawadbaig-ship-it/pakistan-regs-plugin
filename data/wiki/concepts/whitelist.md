# SECP DLA Whitelist

*whitelist*

## Definition

List of permitted Digital Lending Apps run by SECP-licensed NBFCs — apps absent from the list are removed from app stores and blocked from PSP services.

## Why this matters

Pre-launch gating mechanism; coordinated with Google Play and SBP-regulated PSPs.
