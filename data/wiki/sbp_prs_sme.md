# SBP Prudential Regulations - SME Financing (Jan 2025)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Prudential Regulations for SME Financing  
**Date:** 2025-01  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs offering SME finance  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/SME-PRs-Updtd-Jan-2025.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/SME-PRs-Updtd-Jan-2025.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Distinct prudential regime for Small Enterprise (SE) and Medium Enterprise (ME) financing - eased collateral, smaller-ticket, programme-based lending with streamlined documentation.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part I - Definitions of Small Enterprise (SE) and Medium Enterprise (ME) by sales/employees
- Part II - Regulations
- Per-borrower exposure caps (SE and ME)
- Programme-based lending and clean lending limits
- Eligible securities - liquid, pledged stock, plant & machinery, residential / commercial property
- Documentation simplification (KFS, simplified loan agreement)
- Mandatory eCIB check
- Classification and provisioning rules tailored for SME book
- Recovery, rescheduling and write-off
- Linkages with SBP refinance schemes
