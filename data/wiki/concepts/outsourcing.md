# Outsourcing

*outsourcing*

## Definition

Arrangement under which a Regulated Entity has another party perform a function that the RE could otherwise perform itself. Material outsourcing requires Board approval and (for some categories) prior SBP approval.

## Why this matters

Anchors the responsibility-cannot-be-outsourced principle — RE retains accountability regardless of vendor.
