# Asaan Account

*Asaan*

## Definition

Low-friction, low-balance account aimed at financial inclusion — simplified CDD, lower transaction caps, and a clear upgrade path to a full account.

## Why this matters

Often the first account a new-to-bank Pakistani opens; constraints flow from BPRD CL 16/2020.
