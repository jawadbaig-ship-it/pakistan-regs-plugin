# SBP Prudential Regulations - Corporate / Commercial Banking

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Prudential Regulations - Corporate / Commercial Banking  
**Date:** 2024-06 (current)  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-IPF-June-24.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/publications/prudential/PRs-IPF-June-24.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Prudential framework governing corporate and commercial credit by Banks/DFIs - exposure limits, classification, provisioning, security, equity exposures, project finance and large-borrower controls.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part A - Definitions (Bank, Borrower, Equity, FI, Group, Major Shareholder, NPL, Substantial Owner, etc.)
- Part B - Risk Management - Exposure limits per borrower / per group / related parties
- Limit on equity investments and aggregate equity exposure
- Margin requirements and security against advances
- Limit on contingent liabilities
- Restriction on facilities to chief executives, directors and major shareholders
- Part C - Classification of advances (OAEM, Substandard, Doubtful, Loss) by DPD buckets
- Provisioning requirements per classification
- Rescheduling/restructuring rules and tagging
- Write-off pre-conditions and approval
- Disclosure & MIS reporting to SBP
