# Shariah Governance

*Shariah*

## Definition

Governance framework for Islamic Banking Institutions — Shariah Board, Resident Shariah Board Member, Shariah Compliance Department, Internal & External Shariah Audit, product approval.

## Why this matters

Mandatory for full IBIs and Islamic windows of conventional banks.
