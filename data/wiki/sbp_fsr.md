# SBP Financial Stability Review

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Financial Stability Review  
**Date:** Annual  
**Applicable to:** All FIs - strategic and risk planning  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/FSR/index.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/FSR/index.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Annual SBP publication assessing systemic risk - banking, NBFI, payments, household debt, real-economy linkages. Strategic input for risk teams.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Macro-financial assessment
- Banking-sector health
- NBFI and payment-system risks
- Household and corporate debt
- Stress testing and forward-looking risks
