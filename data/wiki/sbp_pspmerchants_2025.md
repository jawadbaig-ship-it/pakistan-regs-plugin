# Facilitation of Merchants Operations

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** PSP&OD Circular 02 of 2025  
**Date:** 2025  
**Applicable to:** PSPs, PSOs, Acquirers, Banks  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2025/C2.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2025/C2.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Streamlines merchant onboarding, settlement, dispute and chargeback for digital-payment acceptance - improves merchant economics on Raast and card rails.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Simplified merchant KYC for low-risk segments
- Settlement timelines (T+1 default)
- Merchant Discount Rate (MDR) caps for small merchants
- Chargeback and dispute SLAs
- Merchant data privacy obligations
- MIS reporting to SBP
