# SBP Consolidated Customer Onboarding Framework 2025

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular 01 of 2025 — Consolidated Customer Onboarding Framework  
**Date:** 2025 (Consolidated)  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs (and their agents/partners performing onboarding)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2025/C1-Consolidated-Customer-Onboarding-Framework.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2025/C1-Consolidated-Customer-Onboarding-Framework.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Single, consolidated framework that supersedes prior fragmented onboarding/KYC guidance for SBP REs. Sets the standard for in-branch, remote and fully digital onboarding of individuals, sole proprietors, partnerships, companies, trusts and foreign clients.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Identity verification — biometric + NADRA Verisys mandatory for natural persons
- In-branch vs remote onboarding — channel-specific controls
- Account types: Asaan, Asaan Digital, Standard, Freelancer, RDA, etc.
- Risk-based CDD; EDD for high-risk customers and PEPs
- Liveness check and document authenticity for digital onboarding
- OTP / e-signature requirements and audit trail
- Beneficial ownership identification for legal entities
- On-going monitoring, periodic KYC refresh, sanction screening
- Reliance on third parties / agent channels — accountability with RE
- Record retention and SBP reporting expectations
