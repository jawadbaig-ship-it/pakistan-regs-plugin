# SECP introduces Self-Assessment Declaration for NBFCs to enrol Digital Lending Apps

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Press Release May 2024  
**Date:** 2024-05-23  
**Applicable to:** Lending NBFCs  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Press-Release-May-23-SECP-Introduces-Self-Assessment-Declaration-for-NBFCs-to-Enroll-Digital-Lending-Apps.pdf](https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Press-Release-May-23-SECP-Introduces-Self-Assessment-Declaration-for-NBFCs-to-Enroll-Digital-Lending-Apps.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Introduces a self-attestation regime for NBFCs to enrol DLAs into the whitelist - speeds onboarding while making NBFCs accountable for compliance representations.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Self-assessment declaration template
- Information required
- Enforcement consequences for misrepresentation
