# NADRA Verisys / Biometric Verification Services

**Regulator:** NADRA  
**Reference:** NADRA Portal  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** All FIs performing identity verification  
**Source:** [https://www.nadra.gov.pk/](https://www.nadra.gov.pk/)

## Purpose & Scope

National database authority providing CNIC verification, biometric verification (Verisys/BVS) and identity attestation - the backbone of digital onboarding and AML CDD.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Verisys (CNIC verification API)
- Biometric Verification System (BVS)
- NADRA service-level agreements and pricing
- Data privacy and access controls
