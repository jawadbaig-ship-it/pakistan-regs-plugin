# Debt Burden Ratio (DBR)

*Debt Burden Ratio*

## Definition

Ratio of total monthly debt servicing to net monthly income — capped by SBP for consumer financing to prevent over-indebtedness.

## Why this matters

Hard limit in underwriting engines for personal loans, credit cards, auto, housing.
