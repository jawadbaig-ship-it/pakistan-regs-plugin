# Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)

*Enhanced Due Diligence*

## Definition

Heightened due-diligence package for customers presenting elevated ML/TF risk — PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions, complex ownership, large unusual transactions. Requires senior-management approval and ongoing monitoring.

## Why this matters

Triggered by risk-rating engines; must be wired into onboarding and periodic-review workflows.
