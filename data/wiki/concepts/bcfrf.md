# Business Conduct & Fair Treatment Regulatory Framework (BC&FRF)

*BC&FRF*

## Definition

SBP's overarching consumer-conduct framework — covers product disclosure, fair treatment, complaint handling, and FI culture.

## Why this matters

Applies across the consumer journey; complaints not resolved internally escalate to Banking Mohtasib.
