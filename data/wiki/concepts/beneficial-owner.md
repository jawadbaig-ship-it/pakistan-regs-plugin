# Beneficial Ownership

*beneficial owner*

## Definition

Natural person(s) who ultimately own or control a customer (≥25% threshold typical for legal entities) — must be identified and verified during CDD.

## Why this matters

Critical for shell-company detection; tied to FATF Recommendations 24/25.
