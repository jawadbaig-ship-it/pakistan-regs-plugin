# Regulatory Sandbox

*sandbox*

## Definition

Time-bound supervised environment for testing innovative financial products with relaxed/customised regulation, customer caps and exit criteria.

## Why this matters

Both SBP and SECP operate sandboxes; cohort-based applications.
