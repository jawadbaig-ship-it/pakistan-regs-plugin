# SBP Revised AML/CFT Regulations for MFBs

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** AC&MFD Circular 03 of 2019 - Revised AML/CFT Regulations for MFBs  
**Date:** 2019 (revised)  
**Applicable to:** All Microfinance Banks  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/acd/2019/C3-Revised-AML-CFT-Regulations-MFBs.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/acd/2019/C3-Revised-AML-CFT-Regulations-MFBs.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

AML/CFT regime tailored to the MFB business model (low-value, financially-included clientele) - simplified-but-risk-based CDD with compensating controls.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part 1 - Risk-based approach
- Part 2 - Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and AML
- Identity verification - NADRA, biometric, supporting documents
- Account opening with NADRA receipt/token
- Simplified CDD for low-risk customers
- EDD for high-risk including PEPs
- Beneficial ownership
- Sanctions screening (UNSC, domestic)
- STR / CTR reporting to FMU
- Record keeping; training; compliance function
