# Technology Risk Management

*TGRMF*

## Definition

Enterprise tech-risk discipline — IT governance, IT strategy, information security, change/release, capacity, monitoring, third-party tech risk.

## Why this matters

Governed for FIs by BPRD C5/2017 and for Payment Institutions by PSD C4/2025.
