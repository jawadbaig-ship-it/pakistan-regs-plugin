# SBP Customers Digital Onboarding Framework (2021)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular 02 of 2021 - Customers Digital Onboarding Framework  
**Date:** 2021-09-15 (revised 2022)  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2021/C2-Annex-A.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2021/C2-Annex-A.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Original digital onboarding framework that enabled fully remote opening of accounts/wallets - precursor to the 2025 Consolidated Framework.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Identity verification - NADRA + biometric/liveness
- Selfie / liveness check workflow
- OTP and e-signature controls
- Risk-based CDD, EDD triggers
- Account types eligible for digital onboarding
- Audit trail and record retention
- Reliance on agents/partners with accountability retained by RE
- Fraud / impersonation controls and SBP reporting
