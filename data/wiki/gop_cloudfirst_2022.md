# Pakistan Cloud First Policy, 2022

**Regulator:** GoP / MoITT  
**Reference:** Pakistan Cloud First Policy 2022  
**Date:** 2022  
**Applicable to:** Government, regulators, FIs (sectoral cascade)  
**Source:** [https://moitt.gov.pk/](https://moitt.gov.pk/)

## Purpose & Scope

National policy preferring cloud adoption for government and providing the broader regulatory direction; complements SBPs CSP outsourcing framework for FIs.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Cloud-first principle for government
- Data classification and residency
- CSP eligibility and procurement
- Sectoral cascading (incl. SBP / SECP)
