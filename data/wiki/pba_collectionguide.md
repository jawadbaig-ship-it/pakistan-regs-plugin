# PBA Guidelines for Collection / Recovery / Repossession Agencies

**Regulator:** PBA  
**Reference:** PBA Guidelines for Collection / Recovery / Repossession Agencies  
**Date:** 2021-03  
**Applicable to:** Banks (and effectively their third-party recovery agencies)

## Purpose & Scope

Industry-standard PBA guidelines for the induction, performance monitoring and conduct of third-party collection, recovery and repossession agencies engaged by Banks - operationalises BPRD C13/2008 Fair Debt Collection.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- 1. Definitions
- 2. Regulatory Framework
- 3. Responsibilities of Financial Institutions
- 4. Code of Conduct (prohibited practices, contact hours, identity disclosure)
- 5. Mechanism for enlistment of agencies with PBA
- Documentation, contracts, audit rights
- Complaint mechanism and disciplinary actions
- Performance monitoring and KPIs
- Repossession of secured assets - lawful procedure
