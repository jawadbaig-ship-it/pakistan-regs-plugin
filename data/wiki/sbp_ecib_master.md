# SBP eCIB Master Circular

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BC&CPD Circular No. 06 of 2021 — eCIB Master Circular  
**Date:** 2021-06 (Master Circular)  
**Applicable to:** All SBP-regulated banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks; private credit bureaus operate complementarily under the Credit Bureaus Act 2015  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2021/C6-Appendix-A-eCIB-Master-Circular.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2021/C6-Appendix-A-eCIB-Master-Circular.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Consolidates rules governing reporting to and use of the SBP electronic Credit Information Bureau (eCIB). Mandatory pre-disbursement check and monthly reporting for all member FIs.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- 1. Purpose of eCIB
- 2. Types of credit reports (consumer / corporate / SME)
- 3. Confidentiality (Section 25-A BCO 1962) — restricted access, no disclosure to third parties
- 4. General reporting instructions — monthly submission of all borrower data (excl. staff)
- 5. Data fields to be reported (CNIC, facility type, outstanding, overdue days, classification)
- 6. Data quality & timeliness requirements; penalties for late/inaccurate reporting
- 7. Borrower dispute-resolution process (FI-level then SBP)
- 8. Pre-disbursement eCIB check is mandatory
- 9. Borrower right to obtain a free credit report annually
