# Credit Bureaus Regulations (Notification)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** Notification BC&CPD/2016  
**Date:** 2016  
**Applicable to:** Private credit bureaus, FIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/notifications/BCCPD/2016/ntf1.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/notifications/BCCPD/2016/ntf1.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Operational regulations under Credit Bureaus Act 2015 - licensing parameters, data security, dispute resolution and audit requirements for private bureaus.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Bureau licensing - capital, governance, fit & proper
- Data security and IT controls
- Permissible data sources and consent
- Borrower dispute resolution
- External audit and SBP supervision
- Penalties
