# National Financial Inclusion Strategy (NFIS) 2024-28

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** NFIS 2024-28  
**Date:** 2024-2028  
**Applicable to:** SBP, SECP, MoF, all FIs (strategic context)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/Finc/index.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/Finc/index.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Whole-of-government strategy to lift financial inclusion - targets for adult account ownership, women inclusion, MSME finance and digital usage. Drives many SBP/SECP regulatory priorities.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Vision and targets (2028)
- Pillars - Access, Usage, Quality
- Priority segments - women, MSMEs, agri, low-income
- Digital financial services as enabler
- Consumer protection and financial literacy
- Implementation governance and KPIs
