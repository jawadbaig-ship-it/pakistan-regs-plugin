# SBP Consumer Protection Department Portal

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SBP Consumer Protection Department (CPD) Portal  
**Date:** Live portal  
**Applicable to:** All SBP REs (and consumers as users of the portal)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/index-cpd.asp](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/index-cpd.asp)

## Purpose & Scope

Top-level entry point to SBP consumer-protection regime — links to BC&FRF, CGHM, Banking Mohtasib, financial literacy, consumer awareness booklets, and the SBP complaint resolution mechanism.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Introduction to Financial Consumer Protection
- Complaint Resolution — CGHM mandate for Banks/MFBs/DFIs
- Banking Mohtasib (Ombudsman) escalation
- Unclaimed Deposits regime
- Laws, regulations, circulars and notifications index
- Publications — Consumer Awareness Booklets
