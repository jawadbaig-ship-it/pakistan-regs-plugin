# Personal Data Protection Bill, 2023 (Final Draft)

**Regulator:** GoP / MoITT  
**Reference:** Personal Data Protection Bill 2023 (Draft)  
**Date:** 2023 (Draft)  
**Applicable to:** All data controllers and processors (incl. FIs, fintechs)  
**Source:** [https://moitt.gov.pk/SiteImage/Misc/files/Final%20Draft%20Personal%20Data%20Protection%20Bill%20May%202023.pdf](https://moitt.gov.pk/SiteImage/Misc/files/Final%20Draft%20Personal%20Data%20Protection%20Bill%20May%202023.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Pakistans forthcoming general data-protection law - introduces controller/processor concepts, lawful bases, data-subject rights, breach notification, and a Data Protection Commission.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Definitions - personal data, sensitive data, controller, processor
- Lawful bases for processing (consent, contract, legal, legitimate interest)
- Data-subject rights - access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection
- Cross-border transfer restrictions and adequacy
- Breach notification timelines
- Establishment and powers of the Data Protection Commission
- Penalties (significant fines)
