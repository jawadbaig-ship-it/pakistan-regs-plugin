# Digital Lending

*digital lending*

## Definition

Loan origination, disbursal, servicing and recovery via digital channels (apps, web). In Pakistan, regulated by SECP for NBFCs and by SBP for banks/MFBs/Digital Banks.

## Why this matters

Most regulatory innovation in 2022–2024 has targeted this segment — KFS, whitelisting, contact-list prohibition, nano-caps.
