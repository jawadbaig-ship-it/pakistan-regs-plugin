# Digital Nano-Lending

*nano-lending*

## Definition

Small-ticket, short-tenor (typically ≤90 days) unsecured consumer lending via apps. Subject to mathematical caps under SECP Circular 10/2023.

## Why this matters

Hard caps on per-borrower exposure, tenor, APR, rollover.
