# SBP Green Banking Guidelines

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** SMEFD/2024 Green Banking  
**Date:** 2024  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/smefd/circulars/2024/Green-Banking.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/smefd/circulars/2024/Green-Banking.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Sectoral guidance for environmentally-sustainable lending - green portfolio definition, ESG risk integration in credit, climate-related disclosures.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Green portfolio definition and taxonomy
- ESG risk integration in credit appraisal
- Environmental due diligence for large exposures
- Climate-related disclosures
- Capacity building and Board oversight
