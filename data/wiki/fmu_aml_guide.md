## Purpose & Scope

Operational guidance from the Financial Monitoring Unit (Pakistans FIU) on STR/CTR identification, red flags, reporting workflow, goAML portal, and FMU expectations.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Pakistan AML/CFT framework overview
- Reporting Entity obligations (CDD, screening, reporting)
- Red flags and typologies (cash-intensive, structuring, trade-based, virtual asset)
- STR vs CTR criteria and thresholds
- goAML portal usage - registration, reporting, feedback
- Tipping-off prohibition and confidentiality
- Sanctions framework (UNSC, schedules)
- Penalties under AML Act 2010
