# Customers Digital Onboarding Framework (Revised April 2022)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD CL 15 of 2022  
**Date:** 2022-04-30  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks, EMIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/CL15-Annex.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2022/CL15-Annex.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Mid-cycle revision of the 2021 Digital Onboarding Framework - tightened liveness, document checks and reliance criteria; superseded by 2025 Consolidated Framework.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Revised liveness and biometric requirements
- Updated document-authenticity checks
- Clarifications on reliance on third parties
- Account-type eligibility refinements
- Audit log and data retention updates
