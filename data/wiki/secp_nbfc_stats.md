# SECP NBFCs Data & Statistics Portal

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP NBFC Statistics Portal  
**Date:** Live  
**Applicable to:** Reference - market intelligence  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/data-and-statistics/nbfcs/](https://www.secp.gov.pk/data-and-statistics/nbfcs/)

## Purpose & Scope

SECP-published statistics on NBFC sector - licensed entities, AUM, lending volumes, digital-lending volumes. Useful for market sizing and benchmarking.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Licensed NBFCs by category
- Sector AUM and trend
- Digital-lending volumes
- Defaults and provisioning
