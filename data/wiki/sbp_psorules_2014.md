# Rules for Payment System Operators (PSOs) and Payment System Providers (PSPs)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** PSO/PSP Rules 2014  
**Date:** 2014  
**Applicable to:** PSOs and PSPs licensed by SBP  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2014/PSO-Rules.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2014/PSO-Rules.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Operating rules under PS&EFT Act 2007 for licensing and supervision of PSOs and PSPs - covers governance, capital, technology and consumer protection.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Eligibility and licensing process
- Minimum capital and ongoing solvency
- Permissible activities
- Governance, fit & proper, AML/CFT
- Technology and security
- Consumer protection and dispute resolution
- Reporting and supervision
