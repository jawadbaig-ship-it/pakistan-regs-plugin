# SBP Consumer Grievances Handling Mechanism (CGHM)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** CPD Circular No. 01 of 2016 — Consumer Grievances Handling Mechanism  
**Date:** 2016-02-29  
**Applicable to:** Banks, MFBs, DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2016/C1-Annex.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2016/C1-Annex.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Mandates a structured, time-bound, accessible complaint handling mechanism inside every Bank, MFB and DFI — with culture, governance, MIS and SBP escalation requirements.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Part 1 — Overarching principles: fair treatment, timely resolution, visibility, accessibility
- Part 2 — Minimum internal set-up: dedicated complaint-handling function, governance
- Policies & procedures, training, MIS for complaint capture
- TAT for acknowledgement and resolution; escalation matrix
- Root-cause analysis and feedback loop into product/process design
- Consumer awareness: visible disclosure of channels (call, web, branch, email)
- Periodic MIS reporting to senior management and to SBP
- Banking Mohtasib (Ombudsman) as final external escalation
