# SBP Measures to Enhance Security of Digital Banking Products (BPRD C4 2023)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular No. 04 of 2023 - Measures to Enhance Security of Digital Banking Products  
**Date:** 2023  
**Applicable to:** Banks, MFBs, DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2023/C4.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2023/C4.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Tightens controls on customer authentication and fraud prevention for internet/mobile banking, digital onboarding and digital payments.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Strengthened login authentication (MFA / biometric / OTP)
- Device binding and anomaly detection
- Transaction velocity and value limits with cooling-off
- Real-time fraud monitoring
- Customer notifications for high-risk events (login from new device, large txn)
- Customer education obligations
- Incident reporting timelines to SBP
- Liability allocation principles for fraud
