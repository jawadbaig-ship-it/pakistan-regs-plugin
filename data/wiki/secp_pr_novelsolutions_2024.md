# SECP enables Digital Lenders to launch Novel Solutions

**Regulator:** SECP  
**Reference:** SECP Press Release May 2024  
**Date:** 2024-05  
**Applicable to:** Industry stakeholders  
**Source:** [https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/SECP-enables-digital-lenders-to-launch-novel-solutions.pdf](https://www.secp.gov.pk/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/SECP-enables-digital-lenders-to-launch-novel-solutions.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Announcement that Circular 12/2024 enables novel digital-lending solutions (e.g., BNPL, secured digital loans) within stricter consumer-protection envelope.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Permitted novel product types
- Conditions for launch
- Linkage with existing PRs and AML/CFT
