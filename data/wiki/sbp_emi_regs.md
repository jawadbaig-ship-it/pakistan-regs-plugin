# SBP Regulations for EMIs

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** PSD Circular — Regulations for Electronic Money Institutions  
**Date:** 2023 (Updated)  
**Applicable to:** Companies licensed as EMIs by SBP under PS&EFT Act 2007  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2023/C3-Enclosure-Regulations-EMIs.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/psd/2023/C3-Enclosure-Regulations-EMIs.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Licensing and operating regime for non-bank issuers of e-money / digital wallets. EMIs may issue e-money against equivalent fiat held in segregated trust accounts; EMIs cannot lend.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- 1. Acronyms / 2. Definitions
- 3. Introduction / 4. Objectives
- 5. Authority (PS&EFT Act 2007) / 6. Applicability
- 7. Scope of activities — issuance, redemption, P2P, P2M, top-up, cross-border subject to FX rules
- 8. Licensing procedure (NOC → Pilot → Commercial)
- Capital requirements and ongoing solvency
- Customer fund safeguarding — trust account ring-fencing
- AML/CFT, CDD tiers, NADRA verification
- Technology, cybersecurity, business continuity, outsourcing controls
- Consumer protection, fees disclosure, transaction limits
