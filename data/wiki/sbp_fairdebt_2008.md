# SBP Fair Debt Collection Guidelines (BPRD C13/2008)

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** BPRD Circular No. 13 of 2008 — Fair Debt Collection Guidelines  
**Date:** 2008-11-03  
**Applicable to:** All Banks and DFIs (extended in spirit to MFBs and NBFC digital lenders via SECP rules)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2008/C13.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2008/C13.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Sets minimum conduct standards for collection and recovery against consumer borrowers — designed to curb harassment and protect borrower dignity.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Pre-collection: complete payment information and 14-day prior notice (SMS/letter) before any field visit
- Prohibited conduct: harassment, contact at inconvenient hours, contact with family members, threats
- Identity disclosure: caller must state name, bank, purpose of call
- All collection calls must be recorded; visit reports retained for at least 6 months
- Third-party recovery agencies: must be enrolled with Pakistan Banks Association (PBA)
- Data privacy: borrower data not to be transferred or misused without consent
- Internal complaint mechanism for grievances against collection staff
- SBP inspection and enforcement
