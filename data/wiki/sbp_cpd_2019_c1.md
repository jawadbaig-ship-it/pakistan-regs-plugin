# Membership of Private Credit Bureaus under Credit Bureaus Act, 2015

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** CPD Circular 01 of 2019  
**Date:** 2019  
**Applicable to:** Banks, MFBs, DFIs  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2019/C1.htm](https://www.sbp.org.pk/cpd/2019/C1.htm)

## Purpose & Scope

Mandates FIs to become members of one or more private credit bureaus licensed under CBA 2015 - complementing eCIB membership for richer credit signals.

## Key Sections / Requirements

- Mandatory membership of at least one licensed private bureau
- Reporting standards and timelines
- Borrower consent
- Confidentiality and data quality
