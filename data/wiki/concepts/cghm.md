# Consumer Grievances Handling Mechanism (CGHM)

*CGHM*

## Definition

Mandated complaint-handling structure inside every Bank/MFB/DFI — dedicated function, TAT, escalation, MIS, root-cause analysis, SBP reporting.

## Why this matters

Operationalises BC&FRF; non-compliance is supervised and penalised.
