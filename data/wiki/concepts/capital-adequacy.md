# Capital Adequacy & Paid-up Capital

*CET1*

## Definition

Minimum CET1/Tier-1/Total capital ratios and minimum paid-up capital by entity type and licence stage.

## Why this matters

Different floors apply to Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks (DRB/DFB), EMIs, NBFCs.
