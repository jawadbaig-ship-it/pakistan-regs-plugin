# SBP Enterprise Technology Governance & Risk Management Framework for FIs

**Regulator:** SBP  
**Reference:** Enterprise Technology Governance & Risk Management Framework for FIs  
**Date:** 2017 (BPRD C5/2017 + amendments)  
**Applicable to:** Banks, DFIs, MFBs, Digital Banks (extended to others by reference)  
**Source:** [https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2017/C5-Annex.pdf](https://www.sbp.org.pk/bprd/2017/C5-Annex.pdf)

## Purpose & Scope

Establishes board/senior-management responsibility for IT governance, IT risk, information security, project governance, change management, BCP and outsourcing of technology in financial institutions.

- 1. Information Technology Governance in FIs

- 2. IT Risk Management

- 5. Business Continuity Planning & Disaster Recovery
- 6. IT Outsourcing & Third Party Risk
- 7. IT Audit & Assurance
